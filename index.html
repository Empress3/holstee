This is your life.
Do what you love, and do it often.
If you don’t like something, change it.
If you don’t like your job, quit.
If you don’t have enough time, stop watching TV.
Travel often.
Getting lost will help you find yourself.
Stop over-analyzing;
life is simple.
All emotions are beautiful.
When you eat, appreciate
every last bite.
Open your
mind, arms, and heart
to new things and people.
We are united in our differences.
Life is about the people you meet
and the things you create with them,
so go out and start creating.
Ask the next person you see
what their passion is
and share your
inspiring dream
with them.
If you're looking for the love of your life, stop.
They will be waiting for you when you
start doing the things you love.
Some opportunities only come once.
Seize them.
Life is short.
Live your dream
and share your passion.
